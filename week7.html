Soldering | Electronics
Capacitor- a passive two-terminal electronic component that stores electrical energy in an electric field.
Transistor- a semiconductor device used to amplify or switch electronic signals and electrical power
Resistor- it controls the flow of current to other components.
LED- a two-lead semiconductor light source
Diode- allows an electric current to pass in one direction (called the diode's forward direction)
Potentiometer- voltage divider used for measuring electric potential
Breadboard- make quick electrical connections between components-
Multimeter- electronic measuring instrument that combines several measurement functions in one unit
Switch- open or close the electrical circuit, turning the flow of electricity on or off.
