There are 6 basic steps to make own pillowcase!
1. Lay out the fabrics for my pillowcase
2. Roll up the fabric like a buritto
3. Fold up the bottom fabric
4. Sew along the raw edges
5. Unroll the burrito and press
6. Trim it to make an appropriate size
