FINAL PROJECT

designing a t-shirt
My final project included 4 significantly different processes
1. Printing supreme logo on my white t-shirt --> used GTX Graphics in order to design the shirt and Brother GT-361 to print on it
2. Embroidering LV logo on its right sleeve --> used Tajima Sai in order to embroider the logo on it.
3. Sewing navy long sleeves on the t-shirt --> sew both sides with sewing machine
4. 3D printing the cloth hanger --> used Maker Bot in order to design it and make it
I spent approximately 7 hours for making my own shirt. 3D printing itself took 2 hours and sewing long sleeves took 2~3 hours
